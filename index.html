About US

Mwafrika Development Initiative (MID) is a Not-for-Profit Organization working towards improving the lives of the people of Baringo County.The organization was registered in 2013 and has its headquartered at Kabarnet town. Since its inception, Mwafrika has implemented in programs that include Reproductive Health (RH), Gender Based Violence (GBV), HIV/AIDS with a bias in EBI prevention and primary eye care, Youth, environmental conservation and climate change ,water and sanitation, food security, advocacy, Governance and Micro Finance and

Since its founding, MID work has affected lives of people in Baringo County, transformed communities, and touched lives of young people of Baringo County.
GOAL

Poverty reduction in the context of sustainable development, through advocacy, research and direct project implementation including the pursuit of the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) and other internationally agreed targets in Baringo Central Sub County, Kenya.

VISION

An empowered, equitable community without poverty and disease where every individual enjoy rights to a life of dignity, development and prosperity.

MISSION

To improve the livelihoods of the poor marginalized groups and communities through economic empowerment, HIV prevention, reproductive health advocacy and food security.
