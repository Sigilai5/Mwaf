About Mwafrika

Mwafrika Development Initiative (MID) is a Not-for-Profit Community Based Organization working towards improving the lives of the people of Baringo County. Registered in 2011 and Headquartered in Kabarnet, MID is constituted as, nonprofit, non-political, organization designed to, build institutions and capacity, and support sustainable development planning. Since its inception, Mwafrika has worked in Health programs that include Reproductive Health (RH), Gender Based Violence (GBV), HIV/AIDS with a bias in EBI prevention and primary eye care), Youth, environment, food security, advocacy, Governance and Micro Finance. Since its founding, MID work has impacted lives of people in Baringo County. The overarching objective of MID is poverty reduction in the context of sustainable development, through advocacy, research and direct project implementation including the pursuit of the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) and other internationally agreed targets in Baringo Central District, Kenya.

Organization Goals

  1. To improve the livelihoods of the poor and marginalized groups and communities through economic empowerment, and improved health care.

  2. increase access to HIV care and treatment services reproductive health and advocacy ,mitigation on Gender Based Violence (GBV)

  3. enhance reproductive health for the people of Baringo County.

  4. raise the economic status of communities in Baringo County
