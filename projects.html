Our Projects

Women Empowerment

The program aims to increase access to quality health care services and support for People Living with HIV/AIDS, for mothers, children, adolescents and youth in the county. The program’s main purpose is to increase uptake of FP/Adolescent with key emphasis on Evidence Based interventions (EBI)

Education

The program aims at increasing access to lasting water, sanitation, clean environment, and hygiene solutions that leads to life-transforming benefits that position communities to begin stepping out of generational poverty.

PMGDISHA

Bring clean water access through right-sized equipment and use of appropriate water sources, manual drilling, and mechanized wells with solar pumps — increasing equitable access for all and improving child well-being

Agricultural Program

Enabling communities to address the underlying causes of conflict and to build meaningful peace at a grassroots level.

Skills Developement

The aim is to strengthening the organization is networking, collaboration and partnership and enhance participation of communities in policy formulation, implementation, monitoring and evaluation. It further Capacity building and advocacy on youth, women, gender, access to clean draining water and clean environment water, food security and livelihoods

Child Welfare

Implement a gender strategy that includes specific and transformative interventions integrated within different program components to promote positive health outcomes for men, women, adolescents and children. These interventions are informed by a gender analysis the project conducted in Baringo County and at the same time create awareness in harmful cultural practices

Old Age Welfare

The program targets adolescent and youth sexual and reproductive health (AYSRH) interventions comprise of a variety of approaches geared towards health promotion and prevention and access to quality adolescent sexual reproductive health information and services.

Free Health Checkup & Vaccination

Install sanitation facilities and provide critical sanitation and hygiene training with the goal of eliminating open defecation and increasing access to basic, improved sanitation and hygiene for all .

Environment

Train local people as mechanics to repair and maintain their water points for longer life, empowering communities to sustainably manage water systems long after World Vision’s work is done
